Product Overview

BioRestore is an innovative digital platform developed by BughawLikha to empower communities and organizations in the protection and restoration of coastal ecosystems. Designed for ease of use, the product seamlessly integrates mangrove health monitoring and marine debris reporting into one unified system. By providing real-time data and visual mapping tools, BioRestore enables users to track, analyze, and report environmental conditions, fostering collective efforts towards a sustainable future.

Product Screenshots

Features

  • Interactive Map: Users can view mangrove coverage, health statuses, and marine debris hotspots in real time.
  • Mangrove Monitoring: Track changes in mangrove health and coverage, offering valuable insights to stakeholders and decision-makers to implement effective restoration strategies.
  • Marine Debris Reporting: Allow community members and organizations to report debris sightings directly via the app, helping to manage and mitigate pollution in coastal areas.
  • Data Analytics: Generate and export detailed reports in CSV or PDF format, providing users with actionable insights for improved decision-making and policy planning.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Designed with both administrators and field users in mind, BioRestore offers an intuitive and easy-to-navigate interface.
